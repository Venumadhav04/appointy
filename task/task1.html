Baba Ramdev falls off elephant while performing yoga, video surfaces

A video of yoga guru Baba Ramdev falling off an elephant while performing yoga has surfaced on social media. The clip shows the elephant moving which disturbs Ramdev’s balance and he falls on the ground. The incident is said to have happened when Ramdev was teaching yoga to saints at an ashram in Uttar Pradesh’s Mathura, according to Times Now.

RR defeat KXIP, register highest-ever successful chase in IPL history

Rajasthan Royals on Sunday chased down the target of 224 in last over against Kings XI Punjab to register the highest-ever successful chase in IPL history. Previously, the highest target successfully chased down in an IPL match was 215 by Rajasthan Royals against Deccan Chargers in 2008. The match witnessed Rajasthan Royals' Rahul Tewatia slam five sixes in an over.

CSK defeat MI in IPL 2020 opener as Dhoni wins his 100th IPL match as CSK captain

CSK defeated MI by five wickets in the last over in IPL 2020 opener in Abu Dhabi on Saturday. The victory is MS Dhoni's 100th win in IPL as the captain of CSK. With the win, CSK ended their five-match losing streak against MI. MI had defeated CSK in all of the four matches between them in 2019.
